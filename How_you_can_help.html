How can we help?

Previous Next


Some of the ways we can help are by donating to charities such as Penguins International, the Global Penguin Society, and the Wildlife Conservation Society.

Penguins, like many other animals, are particularly affected by global warming. Reducing emissions will help penguins and many other animals on their quest for survival.

Here are some easy ways you can reduce emissions:
-Walk or ride a bike when going somewhere close.
-Take public transit or carpool when you can.
-Consume local produce when you can.
-Eat less meat, as cattle release lots of methane, a greenhouse gas.
