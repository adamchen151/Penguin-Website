How are penguins affected by climate change?

Previous Next
Save the Penguins | How Penguins are Affected

While penguins are good at adapting to their environments, climate change and other human impacts are hitting their homes too fast for even them. Some of the biggest threats are habitat loss, pollution, disease, and reduced food availability due to commercial fishing. Climate change causes the sea ice that they depend on for food and shelter is melting before their very eyes.
